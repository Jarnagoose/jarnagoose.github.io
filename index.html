About Us

Jarnagoose Studios started as a group of passionate gamers looking to give back to the industry by rolling up their sleeves and making something for others to enjoy.

But that doesn't tell the whole story.

We want more than good game design. We want to bring authenticity and charm to the games we make. We want to show others that, with a good team, and dash of gumption, games can be fun and imaginative again!

We don't answer to shareholders, we're not dictacted by market trends and spreadsheets, we're just a silly little flock of gamers here at Jarnagoose Studios.


Speaking of our flock, you'd probably like to know a little bit more about our team here at Jarnagoose Studios:

Senior Developer: Serin
Serin has been programming games for fun since he was just a little line of code, Serin was one of the first to join the flock, and has been here since the very beginning.
Narrative Design/Project Manager: Indefinite
Indefinite wears many hats, besides translating the crayon etched ravings of Mr. Jarnagoose into something coherent, Indefinite also serves to keep everyone focused and on task each sprint. If anyone deserves a medal, it's him.
Art Department: Czbc
Somehow holding the entire art department together is one talented individual, CzBC. Legend says 18 years ago he taught Jarnagoose how to sprite his very first piece of art on the computer. Legend also says it was kinda bad.
Composer: J2
Be prepared to have your socks blown off by the musical stylings of our most talented composer. This will be her first game composition, but J2 is no stranger to music, in fact you may have even seen her on tour with one of her bands!
Creative Director/Head: Mr. Jarnagoose
*HONK* The creative driving force behind our studio is an enigma, some say he's more goose than man, some say he's more jar than goose. One thing is for sure, he really likes breadcrusts and wobbling around.

Our Work

Fungo Bungo

Our flagship title is a mushroom-themed, turn-based RPG, set in a fantastical world.

Don't let the cute name confuse you! This adventure seeks to make unlikely heroes out of four mushfolk - our mushroom inspired people - as they journey across the land trying to figure out the origin of a force known only as Corruption.


This game is still in early development with no current release date for demos or alpha feedback. We're building this baby from the ground up, and that takes time!

Until we get to a point where players can demo the first region, enjoy some art of our protagonists and let your imagination run wild! See you out in the forests!
