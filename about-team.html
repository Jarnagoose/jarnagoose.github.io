Meet the Team

At Jarnagoose Studios, we are a passionate team of game developers, artists, and storytellers dedicated to creating immersive gaming experiences. Our diverse backgrounds and shared love for gaming drive us to push the boundaries of creativity and innovation.

Once a silly goose, now the leader of a budding flock, Kyle is our game director, overseeing all aspects of Wandercap Chronicles as the originator of the concept. His list of interests, and thoughts about those interests, are longer than any mortal man can comprehend... but retro, narrative-based games come to mind as inspiration.

His favorite games include:
The Binding of Isaac, EarthBound, Resident Evil (the series), Slay the Spire, and Dark Souls
